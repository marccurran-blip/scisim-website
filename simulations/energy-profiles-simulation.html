Key Concepts
Exothermic (ΔH < 0): Products have less energy than reactants. Energy is released to surroundings — temperature increases.
Activation Energy (Ea): Minimum energy needed for reactant particles to collide successfully and start the reaction.
Catalyst: Provides an alternative reaction pathway with lower Ea. Does NOT change ΔH — same reactants and products.

About SciSim

Free, interactive science simulations built for IGCSE students and teachers.

What is SciSim?

SciSim.co.uk is a collection of interactive browser-based simulations covering the IGCSE Physics, Chemistry and Biology syllabus. Every simulation runs directly in your browser — no downloads, no installations, no sign-up required.

Each simulation is designed to help students visualise abstract scientific concepts, from gas particle behaviour to enzyme kinetics to radioactive decay. They're ideal for classroom demonstrations, independent revision, or simply satisfying your curiosity about how the world works.

For Teachers

SciSim simulations work brilliantly on interactive whiteboards and projectors. Use them for lesson starters, demonstrations, or student-led investigation tasks. Every simulation includes key IGCSE concept definitions at the bottom for quick reference.

Because everything runs in the browser, there's nothing to install on school computers. Just visit the site and start exploring.
